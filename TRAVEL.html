MY EXPERIENCE

My favourite traveling place based on my experience was Cameron Highland. This place was so cold and had varieties of place to go. As an example, there were strawberry farm, tea farm, flower garden and the building in Cameron Highland was also beautiful. Those place was really great because we can experiencing picking strawberry, eat the strawberry on the spot and also had a very fresh tea. The hotel prices at Cameron Highland was so affordable and the transportation around the place was also great. Which mean, if we do not want to drive we can just utilized the transportation there and enjoying the trips to all places in Cameron Highland with no worries of getting lost.
